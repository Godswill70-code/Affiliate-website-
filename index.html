Start Here: What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a simple way to earn money by recommending products online. You don’t need to create your own product — you just share a unique link, and when someone buys, you earn a commission.

Why Affiliate Marketing is Perfect for Beginners

  • No tech skills needed
  • Free to start
  • Works while you sleep
  • Flexible — do it anytime, anywhere

Free Tools to Help You Succeed

Here are some beginner-friendly tools:

  • CapCut – Edit short videos easily
  • Bit.ly – Shorten your affiliate links
  • ChatGPT – Get help writing your content
